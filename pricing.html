Pricing questions

The fine print, in plain English

What counts as an “order”?

Any completed checkout while Brix is installed — whether or not a Brix offer touched it. If you’re between plan sizes, we count the trailing 30 days, not calendar months.

What happens if I go over my order limit?

Nothing breaks. Your offers keep running for the rest of the cycle, and we email you to suggest the next plan — 50 orders/month on Starter, 500 on Pro, unlimited on Scale. We never switch plans or charge you without your say-so.

Do I need a credit card to start?

Not for Starter — it’s free forever. Pro and Scale start with a free trial and then bill monthly through Shopify Billing, so there’s no separate card to enter and you can cancel from your Shopify admin anytime.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, in either direction, from the Brix dashboard. Upgrades apply immediately; downgrades apply at the start of your next billing cycle. Your offers, bundles and analytics carry over either way.
